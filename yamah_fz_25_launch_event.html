YAMAHA NEWLY LAUNCH- ALL NEW POWERFULL FZ25 DEBUTS IN INDIA!

Much awaited bike from Yahama India, finally was launched in New Delhi, India on 24th January, 2017 from its successful and popular FZ series. The new model FZ25 has been developed with a newly designed air-cooled, 249 cc, 4 stroke, SOHC, 2-valve, single-cylinder, high torque fuel injected engine on a light weighted frame enabling the joy of control as the rider intends.

The design emphasizes both the senses of mass forward layout and downforce, evoking the powerful muscles of an athlete.

The wide speculations finally made it to the much awaited grand launch on 24th of January in Pullman Hotel, New Delhi as Bollywood heartthrob John Abraham revealed the new model ton public applause and delight.

Based on a reliable 249cc single-cylinder engine proven on sport bikes for the Japanese market, the model's engine has been fine-tuned and optimized for the Indian traffic environment. With its maximum power output of 20.9 PS @ 8000 rpm and maximum torque of 20 Nm @ 6000 rpm, the new 249 cc model also offers an impressive array of new features including the LED headlight and an LCD instrument cluster with a high-quality look evoking a sense of innovation, a mudguard that moves with the swing arm, wheels with a spoke design like that of MT Series bikes and racing style footpads making it an irresistible option as the top end model of FZ series.

With the new launch, the company is confident that it will be able to make new inroads in the customer base especially those who wants to step up from the deluxe segment to premium. Speaking on the occasion, Mr Masaki Asano, Managing Director, Yahama Motor India Sales Pvt. Ltd, said, "The FZ series has been a great performer ever since its launch in 2008. FZ series performed very well in India and crossed 1.3 million sales since its launch and has developed as a strong brand in the deluxe segment. Now the deluxe segment customers can step up to a strong brand in the deluxe segment.

Yahama's Blue Core Technology

Implemented by Yahama Motor Co., Ltd, Japan on various model, "Blue Core" is a next-generation engine development ideal for various models that take on the following three points:-

- Increasing combustion efficiency
- Increasing cooling efficiency
- Reducing power loss

Key features of the New FZ25



- Good Mileage (43 kmpl) & Excellent Engine Performance and Improvement in fuel economy.
- First LED headlight on a 250cc model in India.
- Maximum power output of 20.9 PS@ 8000 rpm and maximum torque of 20.0 Nm @ 6000 rpm.
- Outstanding balance of acceleration performance.
- Its lightness i.e just 148 kgs with fuel, together with its riding positon that conveys the model's newness and freshness while bringing out its street fighter character.
- New front and rear suspension.
